Opportunities

Our group welcomes motivated students and researchers with interest in soft matter, biophysics, and active matter.

Postdoctoral Position

Our group currently has one open postdoctoral position.
Candidates with a PhD in soft matter physics or biophysics, and strong experience in computational/theoretical modeling, are encouraged to apply.

Email your CV + list of publications to adasanna@iisermohali.ac.in.

PhD Positions

We recruit PhD students through the IISER Mohali PhD admission process. Candidates with strong backgrounds in physics, applied mathematics, or engineering — and interest in theoretical or computational soft matter — are encouraged to apply.

Skills preferred: statistical physics, classical mechanics, numerical methods, C/C++, Python, Linux.

How to apply:
Visit the IISER Mohali website for PhD admission notifications: IISER Mohali

MS Thesis

We regularly host MS thesis students from IISER Mohali. Work involves computational modeling, simulation techniques, and theoretical analysis of biological and soft matter systems.

To join: email the PI briefly describing your background and interests.
